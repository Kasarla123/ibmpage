About IBM

IBM, or International Business Machines Corporation, is a global technology and consulting company headquartered in Armonk, New York, USA. Established in 1911, IBM has a rich history of innovation and has played a significant role in shaping the digital era.

At IBM, our mission is to lead in the creation, development, and manufacture of the industry's most advanced information technologies, including computer systems, software, networking systems, storage devices, and microelectronics. We provide companies with innovative solutions to complex business challenges, helping them adapt and thrive in the ever-changing digital landscape.

With a presence in over 170 countries and regions, IBM serves clients across various industries, including healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, and government. Our diverse portfolio of products and services encompasses artificial intelligence, cloud computing, blockchain technology, cybersecurity, and more.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence is deeply ingrained in our culture. We invest heavily in research and development to push the boundaries of what's possible and drive meaningful advancements in technology. Additionally, we prioritize sustainability and corporate responsibility, striving to make a positive impact on society and the environment.

IBM's values of trust, responsibility, innovation, and inclusivity guide our actions and decisions every day. We believe in the power of technology to transform lives, businesses, and the world, and we're dedicated to helping our clients harness that power to achieve their goals.

Learn more about IBM, explore our solutions, and discover how we can help you unlock new possibilities and drive success in the digital age.
